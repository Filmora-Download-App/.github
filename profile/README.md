## 🎯 Getting Started
1. Open Filmora and create a new project, choosing the aspect ratio that matches your target (16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for short-form social, 1:1 for square posts).
2. Drag your video, image, and audio files into the media bin, then drop the clips onto the timeline in the order you want them to play.
3. Trim and split clips with the playhead, then add transitions between cuts to smooth the flow.
4. Apply titles, color presets, effects, and background music from the built-in asset library, and use the AI tools when you need to isolate a subject, track motion, or clean up audio.
5. Click **Export**, pick a preset for your target platform or device, and save the finished video.
